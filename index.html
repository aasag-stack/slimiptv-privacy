Privacy Policy for Slim IP TV

Last Updated: January 28, 2025

Overview

Slim IP TV ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data stored by Slim IP TV remains on your device:

  • IPTV playlist URLs and credentials you provide
  • Your favorite channels
  • Watch history (recently watched channels)
  • App preferences (language, theme, folder organization)
  • Hidden channels list

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers (we don't have servers!)
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is deleted if you delete the app

Third-Party Services

The app connects only to IPTV services YOU provide (playlist URLs, Xtream Codes servers). We do not control, monitor, or have access to these connections.

No Tracking

We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Tracking cookies
  • Advertising networks
  • Data collection tools

Content

Slim IP TV is a player application only. We do not provide, host, or endorse any content. Users are responsible for their own IPTV service subscriptions and content access.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not target children.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.

Contact

For questions about privacy: support.slimiptv@gmail.com

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all app data by deleting the app
  • Control what data you store by managing app settings
  • Stop using the app at any time

Summary

Slim IP TV stores data only on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or access any of your information. Your privacy is completely protected.
